About Us

Here in Mary Bell Township we strive to meet an expectation of exellence in all we do. We pride ourself on providing care for citizens who need it, and we are always pushing to do more. We are always innovating and growing as a community, so we hope you will join us in creating a brighter future for our residents.

Who We Are

Mary Bell is a city of medical and civic advancement. As the city with the largest anemic population in the USA, we care about improving the lives of those who suffer from anemia as well as other illnesses and disorders. We host a number of fundraisers, blood drives, and scientific grants year-round, all aimed at facilitating medical advances. Mary Bell is a medical pioneer in the state of Texas. In Mary Bell we also strive to keep our facilities and public spaces as modern and advanced as possible.
